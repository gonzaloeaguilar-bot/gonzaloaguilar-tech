### Stat 3 — Cost overrun and timeline reality for SMEs

**Data point:** AI implementation for SMEs requires $200,000–$500,000 over five years on the higher end, but hidden costs inflate total AI ownership 200–400% beyond initial vendor quotes for enterprise deployments. **Source:** SmartDev / Coherent Solutions cost breakdown analysis, 2024–2025. https://smartdev.com/gen-ai-implementation-cost-sme/ and https://www.coherentsolutions.com/insights/ai-development-cost-estimation-pricing-structure-roi
