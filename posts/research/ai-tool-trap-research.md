**Source:** S&P Global Market Intelligence survey of 1,000+ enterprises across North America and Europe, reported by NTT DATA Group (2025). https://www.nttdata.com/global/en/insights/focus/2024/between-70-85p-of-genai-deployment-efforts-are-failing

**Exact sentence to replace:**

**Replace with:**

### Stat 2 — ROI measurement failure

**Data point:** 66% of companies struggle to establish ROI metrics for AI initiatives, which is a primary driver of wasted spend and premature tool abandonment.

**Source:** Fullview / multiple industry surveys compiled, citing data consistent with McKinsey and Gartner findings. https://www.fullview.io/blog/ai-statistics

**Where to insert:** Question 2 section ("How Will I Measure Success?"), after the opening paragraph explaining that most business owners skip this question.

**Exact sentence to add (after "Most business owners skip this question entirely."):**
> It's a systemic problem: 66% of companies report struggling to establish any ROI metrics for their AI tools at all — meaning they have no way to know whether the tool is helping or hurting (industry survey data, 2025).
