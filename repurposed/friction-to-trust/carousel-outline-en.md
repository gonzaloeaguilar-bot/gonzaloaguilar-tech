# Carousel: The Hidden Friction That Kills Your Customer Journey

## Slide 1: Hook
**Your website looks great, but customers disappear faster than free Costco samples 🛒**
Hidden friction points in your customer journey are silently killing conversions — and you probably don't even see them.

## Slide 2: The Problem
**70% of online purchases are abandoned due to poor user experience 📉**
Every friction point is a conversion killer turning interested prospects into frustrated abandoners who associate your brand with hassle, not solutions.

## Slide 3: What is Hidden Friction?
**It's like pebbles in your shoe — small annoyances that compound into deal-breaking frustrations 👠**
Any unnecessary difficulty, confusion, or hesitation customers experience while trying to buy from you.

## Slide 4: The Top 5 Friction Killers
**These invisible obstacles are costing you money right now 💸**
Information gaps, trust deficits, decision fatigue, mobile unfriendliness, and unclear value propositions.

## Slide 5: The Solution Framework
**Introducing the Friction-to-Trust Pipeline: Detect → Diagnose → Design → Deploy 🔄**
Don't just remove obstacles — strategically replace them with confidence boosters that build trust.

## Slide 6: Detect Like a Pro
**Walk through your own buying process monthly and time yourself ⏱️**
Better yet, watch someone else try to buy from you. Their hesitations reveal your friction points better than any analytics dashboard.

## Slide 7: Design Trust-Building Swaps
**Replace confusing pricing with clear cost breakdowns. Replace generic forms with multiple contact options. 🔄**
Transparency beats perfection. Progress beats complexity. Proof beats promises.

## Slide 8: Deploy Systematically
**Change one thing at a time and measure what matters 📊**
Focus on conversion rates, time on key pages, and customer feedback — not vanity metrics that don't connect to revenue.

## Slide 9: Your Competitive Advantage
**Every friction point you transform becomes a competitive edge 🎯**
While competitors focus on flashy features, you're building frictionless experiences that actually convert.
