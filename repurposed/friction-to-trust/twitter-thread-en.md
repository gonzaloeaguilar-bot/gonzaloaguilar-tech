1/ Your website looks great. Your product is solid. Your prices are competitive. 

So why are customers disappearing faster than free samples at Costco? 🛒

The answer: Hidden friction is silently killing your conversions. Here's how to fix it 👇

2/ Hidden friction = any unnecessary difficulty your customers face while trying to buy from you.

Think pebbles in your shoe. One? Annoying. Five? You're shopping elsewhere.

70% of online purchases are abandoned due to poor UX. That's not just lost sales — that's lost customers.

3/ Introducing the Friction-to-Trust Pipeline:
• DETECT — Find the invisible obstacles  
• DIAGNOSE — Understand why they exist
• DESIGN — Create trust-building alternatives
• DEPLOY — Implement and measure

Don't just remove friction. Transform it into competitive advantage.

4/ Top 5 friction killers hiding in your customer journey:

🔍 Information gaps (unclear pricing, missing details)
🤝 Trust deficits (no testimonials, weak credibility signals)  
🧠 Decision fatigue (too many choices/steps)
📱 Mobile unfriendliness (60% of traffic!)
💡 Unclear value prop (visitors can't grasp what you do)

5/ Here's the secret: Replace friction with TRUST signals

❌ Confusing pricing → ✅ Clear cost breakdowns
❌ Generic contact form → ✅ Multiple options + response times  
❌ Boring descriptions → ✅ Benefit-focused copy
❌ Empty testimonials → ✅ Specific success stories with proof

6/ Pro tip: Walk through your own buying process monthly. 

Time yourself. Count clicks. Note every confusion moment.

Better yet? Watch someone else try to buy from you. Their hesitations reveal friction better than any analytics dashboard.

7/ Remember: Every friction point you eliminate becomes a competitive advantage.

While competitors focus on flashy features, you're building frictionless experiences that convert.
