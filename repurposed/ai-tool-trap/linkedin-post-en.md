🤔 Ever wonder why 67% of small businesses abandon their AI tools within six months?

It's not because AI doesn't work. It's because we're buying backwards — grabbing shiny solutions before we understand our actual problems.

I just watched another business owner spend $4,800 on three AI tools last year. Today? Only one gets used, and they can't even prove it's worth the monthly fee.

Here's the thing: most of us discover AI tools the same way we impulse-buy at checkout. LinkedIn post about ChatGPT writing campaigns? Sign up. Competitor mentions AI customer service? Free trial. Nephew shows logo-making app? Monthly subscription.

**The result?** $3,000-$8,000 annually in unused software that creates "tool fatigue" across your team.

The fix is simple: **The Three Questions Framework**

Before spending another dollar, ask:
1. What SPECIFIC problem am I solving?
2. How will I measure success?
3. What's my backup plan if this doesn't work?

Not "I want AI to help with marketing" — but "I spend 4 hours every Tuesday writing social posts when I need that time for client calls."

Not "better engagement" — but "increase social engagement by 25% in 90 days."

Not "the tool seems reliable" — but "monthly data exports plus Excel backups of key workflows."

**Tranquilo — yo te explico:** If you can't answer all three questions clearly, you're not ready to buy. Period.
