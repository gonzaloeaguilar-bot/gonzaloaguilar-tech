**Headline:** "The average small business wastes $3,000-$8,000 annually"

## Slide 4: Introducing the Framework
**Headline:** "The Three Questions Framework: Think like an investor, not a consumer"
**Content:** Before spending another dollar on AI, ask these three questions in this exact order. It's your filter system for smart decisions.

## Slide 5: Question 1
**Headline:** "What SPECIFIC problem am I solving?"
**Content:** Wrong: "I want AI to help with marketing." Right: "I spend 4 hours every Tuesday writing social posts—I need that time for client calls."

## Slide 6: Question 2
**Headline:** "How will I measure SUCCESS?"
**Content:** Pick a number that makes this purchase obviously worth it. Time saved? Quality improved? Revenue increased? No number = not ready to buy.

## Slide 7: Question 3
**Headline:** "What's my backup plan if this doesn't work?"
**Content:** Can you export your data? Cancel easily? Run your business without this tool? Always have a Plan B before committing.

## Slide 8: The Filter in Action
**Headline:** "Most AI tools fail at least one question"
**Content:** Customer service AI that cuts response time from 6 hours to 2? Passes all three. Social media tool for "better content"? Fails them all.

**Headline:** "Track your problem for 30 days before buying anything"
**Content:** You can't improve what you don't measure. This simple exercise eliminates 80% of unnecessary AI purchases.
