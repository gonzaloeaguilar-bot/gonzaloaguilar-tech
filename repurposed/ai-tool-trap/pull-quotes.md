Here are 5 standalone pull quotes that work perfectly as social media snippets:

1. **"Most business owners buy AI tools the same way they impulse-buy at the grocery store—they grab what looks appealing without checking if they need it."** This scattershot approach costs small businesses $3,000-$8,000 annually in unused subscriptions.

2. **"If you can't explain the problem in one sentence to your grandmother, you don't understand it well enough to buy a solution."** Vague problem definitions lead to buying multiple overlapping tools that don't move your business forward.

3. **"Before you buy, write down the number that would make this purchase obviously worth it. If you can't pick a number, you're not ready to buy."** Without clear success metrics, you'll keep paying for tools that feel useful but don't improve your bottom line.

4. **"You can't improve what you don't measure. Thirty days of data beats months of guessing."** This simple exercise eliminates 80% of unnecessary AI purchases before you waste money on solutions you don't need.

5. **"AI tools should amplify your existing strengths, not fix fundamental business problems."** If your sales process is broken, AI won't fix it. Start with solid foundations, then add smart technology.
