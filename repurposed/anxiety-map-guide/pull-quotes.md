Here are 5 standalone pull quotes that work as social media snippets:

1. **"Your customers are scared of you."** Not personally — they're scared of making the wrong choice, wasting money, or looking foolish. Every buying decision triggers a cascade of worries that can kill your sale faster than a bad Yelp review.

2. **"Fear drives more decisions than desire."** The most expensive mistake I see small business owners make is assuming their customers think like they do. You know your business is legitimate — your customer doesn't know any of this yet.

3. **"When you don't address customer anxiety, you're essentially running a business with the parking brake on."** Every anxious customer represents lost revenue, but the real damage goes deeper — you waste money on traffic that doesn't convert.

4. **"Don't make customers hunt for reassurance."** Use your anxiety map to create FAQ sections, product descriptions, and sales materials that address common worries upfront. Anticipate questions before they're asked.

5. **"The goal isn't to eliminate all customer anxiety — it's to address the fears that matter most to your business success."** Map their anxiety first, then build solutions that customers actually want to buy.
