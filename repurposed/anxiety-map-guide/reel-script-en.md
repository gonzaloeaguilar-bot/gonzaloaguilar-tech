**60-Second Reel Script: "Map Your Customer's Anxiety"**

[VISUAL: Split screen - stressed person shopping online vs. confused business owner]
**HOOK (3s):**
"Your customers are scared of you. Here's why that's killing your sales..."

[VISUAL: Graph showing dropping conversion rates]
**PROBLEM (10s):**
"Most small business owners build first, research later. They create websites and products based on what makes sense to THEM, then wonder why customers aren't buying. The truth? You're solving the wrong puzzle. Fear drives more decisions than desire, and anxious customers simply don't convert."

[VISUAL: Simple animated framework with three phases]
**FRAMEWORK (25s):**
"Here's my Anxiety Map framework. Phase 1: Discovery — survey recent customers, mine support emails, track sales objections. Ask 'What almost stopped you from buying?' Phase 2: Categorization — sort fears into buckets: financial, performance, security, social, process, and support anxieties. Phase 3: Prioritization — map fears to your funnel, estimate impact, and tackle high-impact, low-effort fixes first."

[VISUAL: Before/after conversion screenshots]
**MIDDLE (15s):**
"Then build experiences that address fears upfront. Add trust badges for security worries. Show clear return policies for financial fears. Use progress indicators for process anxiety. Don't make customers hunt for reassurance."
