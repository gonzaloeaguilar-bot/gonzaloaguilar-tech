1/🧵 You've heard it everywhere: "AI will transform your business!" But here's what nobody's telling you — jumping into AI without being ready is like building a second floor before your foundation is solid. Most small businesses waste money and frustrate teams by rushing in unprepared.

2/🧵 Before you spend a dime on AI tools, ask yourself these 3 honest questions:
• Can you clearly describe what problem AI will solve? (Hint: "make everything better" isn't specific enough)
• Do you have organized data and processes for AI to work with?
• Is your team ready to change workflows?

3/🧵 RED FLAG ALERT 🚩
Your business isn't ready for AI if:
- You're behind on basic practices like regular bookkeeping
- Your team struggles with current technology
- Important data is scattered across sticky notes and random spreadsheets
- You see AI as "set it and forget it"

4/🧵 The infrastructure reality check: AI tools need reliable internet, modern devices, and centralized data. If your customer info is in one system, sales data in another, and inventory somewhere else, AI can't connect the dots to help you. Fix the foundation first.

5/🧵 Here's the truth about AI budgeting: If a tool costs $50/month, you should be comfortable paying $300 upfront. Why? Real AI value takes months to materialize as teams learn and processes adjust. Expecting immediate ROI leads to abandoned tools and wasted money.

6/🧵 The 80/20 rule of AI success: It's 20% technology, 80% change management. You need at least one "tech-curious" person on your team and leadership committed to supporting the transition. Without this, expensive tools become expensive digital paperweights.
