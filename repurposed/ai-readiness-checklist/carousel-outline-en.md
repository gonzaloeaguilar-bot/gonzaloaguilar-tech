# AI Readiness Carousel Outline

## Slide 1: Hook
**"Your business isn't ready for AI (and that's totally fine)"**
Everyone's pushing AI adoption, but most small businesses jump in too early and waste money. Here's how to know if you're actually ready.

## Slide 2: The Reality Check
**"Three questions that reveal everything"**
Can you describe the specific problem AI should solve? Do you have organized data for AI to work with? Is your team ready to change workflows?

## Slide 3: Foundation First
**"You can't build the second floor without solid ground"**
Before AI can help, your core processes need to be documented and your success metrics clearly defined. No shortcuts here.

## Slide 4: Data Disaster Zone
**"Scattered data = scattered results"**
If your customer info lives on sticky notes and random spreadsheets, AI can't connect the dots. Centralize first, automate second.

## Slide 5: Team Reality
**"Your overwhelmed team can't handle more complexity"**
AI adoption is 20% technology, 80% change management. If your people are already stressed, new tools will hurt more than help.

## Slide 6: Budget Truth
**"Plan for 6 months, not 6 weeks"**
Real AI implementation takes months to show value. If you're counting on immediate ROI, you'll abandon tools before they pay off.

## Slide 7: Red Flags to Watch
**"Stop if you see these warning signs"**
You're behind on basic bookkeeping, hoping AI will fix fundamental business problems, or looking for "set it and forget it" solutions.

## Slide 8: The Smart Path Forward
**"Start small, build steady, win consistently"**
Address foundation gaps first. When ready, pick one specific problem and experiment gradually. No prize for being first, huge value in being effective.
