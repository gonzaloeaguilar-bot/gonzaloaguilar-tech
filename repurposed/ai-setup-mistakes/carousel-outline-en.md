# AI Implementation Carousel: Don't Let These Mistakes Cost You Thousands

## Slide 1: Hook
**"Your AI investment is probably failing (and it's not your fault)"**
Most small businesses waste thousands on AI tools that never deliver results. Here are the 5 costly mistakes keeping you stuck.

## Slide 2: The Hidden Framework
**"The Friction-to-Trust Pipeline: Why AI feels hard before it gets easy"**
Every successful AI tool creates initial friction (confusion, extra work) before building trust (reliable results, time savings). Most businesses quit during the friction phase.

## Slide 3: Mistake #1
**"Starting with complex solutions = guaranteed failure"**
Jumping to sophisticated AI platforms before mastering simple tools is like buying a professional espresso machine when you just need better coffee. Start small, win big.

## Slide 4: Mistake #2
**"Garbage data in = garbage results out"**
AI tools are only as good as your data foundation. Clean, organized information first—then automation that actually works.

## Slide 5: Mistake #3
**"Vague goals = wasted money"**
"Improve efficiency" isn't a success metric. Define specific, measurable outcomes: "reduce response time from 4 hours to 1 hour" gives you clear winning signals.

## Slide 6: Mistake #4
**"Great tools + untrained team = expensive failure"**
Even the best AI is useless if your team doesn't know how to use it. Budget for training upfront, create internal champions, build momentum through the friction phase.

## Slide 7: Mistake #5
**"Automating everything at once creates chaos, not improvement"**
Multiple simultaneous AI implementations compound friction phases. Master one tool completely before expanding to the next area.

## Slide 8: The Solution
**"Your AI success roadmap: Simple → Foundation → Training → Scale"**
Start with one simple tool. Prepare clean data and clear metrics. Invest in team training. Embrace initial friction. Scale gradually after proven wins.
